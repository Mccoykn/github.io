Our Mission

Our mission is to "Declare his glory among the nations, his marvelous deeds among all people" 1 Chronicles 16:24. We want to allow all people to carry the Lord with them everywhere they go.

The Story Behind The Pure Devotion

I am someone who loves fashion. Creating was just a habit. It started with a love for sewing, then lead to me creating my own clothes. People began to ask me where I got my clothes from, not knowing I made them myself. That is when the brand began. This brand turned into a prayer then it turned into reality. I started making pieces and adding scriptures to them. People fell in love and requested more. I want to design clothes that people can afford, made with high quality materials. I wanted to create a brand that people could relate to and create clothes that people actually want to wear, not just basic Christian apparel. Around 2026 I built the brand from the ground up. I started online and created a lot of traffic. I built a brand for myself as well on social media. Then opened my first shop in 2030. It has been blessing and much more since then.
